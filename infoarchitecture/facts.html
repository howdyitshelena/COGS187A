Facts about the University of Leicester

We’re a leading UK university committed to international excellence through the creation of world changing research and high quality, inspirational teaching. Indeed, we were awarded the title of University of the Year by the Times Higher Education. We’re rightfully proud of our elite status and we consistently rank in the top 20 universities.


2014

We are the only university to win seven consecutive Times Higher Awards. In awarding us 'University of the Year', Times Higher Education applauded Leicester’s very different approach, describing us as “elite without being elitist”.

This is us

The University of Leicester is a leading UK University committed to international excellence through the creation of world changing research and high quality, inspirational teaching. Leicester is consistently one of the most socially inclusive of the UK’s top 20 universities with a long-standing commitment to providing fairer and equal access to higher education.
