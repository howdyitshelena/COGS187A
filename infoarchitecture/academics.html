Academics Excellence... Starts here

The University of Leicester is a leading UK University committed to international excellence through the creation of world changing research and high quality, inspirational teaching. Leicester is consistently one of the most socially inclusive of the UK’s top 20 universities with a long-standing commitment to providing fairer and equal access to higher education.


Schools & Colleges

Our four colleges offer a wide array of degree programs that allows students to pursuit.

  • College of Arts, Humanities and Law
  • College of Medicine, Biological Sciences and Psychology
  • College of Science and Engineering
  • College of Social Science

Graduate/Professional Programmes

As a major research institution, we can offer postgraduate programmes in a wide range of fields and you benefit from working with internationally recognised scholars who are at the frontiers of their disciplines.

International Programmes

We welcome students from more than 70 countries. If you are a student at a university in another country, you may also be able to study here as a Study Abroad or Exchange student.

Distance Learning

We are one of the biggest suppliers of Higher Education distance learning courses in the UK. We offer courses through Distance Learning study and Continuing Professional Development (CPD). Unlike campus-based courses, Distance Learning may have one, two, three or four possible start-dates throughout the year.

Why study with us?

At Leicester, we’re continually striving to use our academic expertise to increase our understanding of the world, so that we might change it for the better. And we’ve made many important discoveries along the way, from the technique of DNA fingerprinting, to unearthing a lost city from an ancient civilisation in Libya – an astonishing find that could re-write history.
