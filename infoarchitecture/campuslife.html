Be Part of the Community

Learn about what the University of Leicester can offer that can enrich your life experience during your time here with us. There are countless events, talks and student organized clubs available for students to join or attend.


Culture

Our campus is diverse. We have students from over 70 countries attending our university, and you will have the opportunity to be exposed to different cultures around the world.

Recreation

Sport and dinning services are available on campus for students, staffs and guesses. The University organizes events in a regular basis which welcome you to attend. There are also plenty of student-run clubs and societies that you are welcome to join!

Health & Safety

Your well-being is our top priority. There are a range of services and facilities available to members of staff at the University to encourage health and well-being.
